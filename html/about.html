About 80808 Vinyls


Welcome to our vinyl store, a haven for music enthusiasts and lovers of the alternative scene. We pride ourselves on curating a diverse selection of alternative records, spanning genres such as indie, punk, rock, electronic, and everything in between. With an unwavering passion for music, we aim to provide a haven for those seeking a tangible connection to their favorite artists and a nostalgic journey through the analog sounds of the past. At our store, we understand the unparalleled magic that lies within the grooves of a vinyl record. We believe that music should be experienced in its purest form, free from the confines of digital compression. Our carefully curated collection features both new releases and timeless classics, allowing you to discover hidden gems or rediscover the artists who shaped the alternative music landscape.

But it doesn't end with just the records. We also offer a range of high-quality record players and audio equipment to enhance your listening experience. Whether you're a seasoned vinyl enthusiast or a newcomer to the world of analog sound, our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to guide you through the options and help you find the perfect setup that suits your preferences and budget. Step into our vinyl store and immerse yourself in the warm, rich tones that only vinyl can offer. Whether you're searching for your favorite band's latest release or embarking on a journey of musical exploration, we're here to celebrate the alternative sound and create a space where music lovers can connect, discover, and cherish the beauty of vinyl records and record players.
